VAC appeals and reconsiderations

If your VAC claim was denied or you disagree with a decision, we help you understand your options — reconsiderations, departmental reviews, and Veterans Review and Appeal Board hearings.

·Decision letter walkthrough
·Reconsideration support

02 — Claims Learn more →

First-time VAC disability claims

We help you put together a clear, complete first-time application for VAC Disability Benefits — connecting your service history to your current health, with strong documentation.

·Eligibility assessment
·Document checklist tailored to your case
·Submission review before filing
03 — Reassessment Learn more →

Apply for a higher VAC disability rating

If your service-related condition has worsened or you've received new medical evidence, you may qualify for a higher rating. We help you assess whether a reassessment is worthwhile and prepare a strong application.

·Medical evidence review
·Comparative case insight
·Plain-language application support
