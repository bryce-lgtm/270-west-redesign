Services / Support

Ongoing support for veterans, families, and caregivers

We don't disappear after the application.

Whether you're waiting on a decision, navigating a new program, or supporting a loved one — we're here for the long stretch.

01 — Common situations

When veterans and families come to us for support

Support work isn't always about paperwork. Sometimes it's just about knowing what's next.

Situation 01

"I got my decision letter — what does it actually mean?"

We sit with you and translate, plain language, no rushing.

Situation 02

"I need help finding the right healthcare provider."

We connect you with medical professionals who understand veteran-specific assessments and supporting documentation.

Situation 03

"Getting to appointments is hard for me."

We help coordinate transportation to medical appointments and assessments — so logistics don't slow your claim down.

Situation 04

"My partner is a veteran and I'm trying to help."

Family members are often the ones doing the navigating. We help you, too.

Situation 05

"I need to access the Caregiver Recognition Benefit."

We help eligible caregivers understand and apply for the programs that recognize their work.

Situation 06

"I'm not sure who to talk to at VAC."

We can guide your communication with Veterans Affairs on your behalf — so you stay informed without doing all the back-and-forth.
