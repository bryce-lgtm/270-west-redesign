Services / Reassessment

VAC reassessment and rating-increase support

When your situation changes, your rating can too.

If your service-related condition has worsened, or new medical evidence has come to light, you may qualify for a higher rating. We help you make the case.

01 — Common situations

When veterans come to us for reassessments

Reassessments are common — your health changes over time, and your rating should reflect that.

Situation 01

"My condition has gotten worse."

If you have new medical evidence, a reassessment may be the right next step. We help you decide.

Situation 02

"I have a new diagnosis related to my service."

New conditions sometimes warrant a new claim rather than a reassessment. We help you tell the difference.

Situation 03

"I'm not sure it's worth the paperwork."

We do a quick honest review and tell you if it is. No pressure to proceed.

02 — Our approach

How we help with VAC reassessments

Reassessments succeed when the change in your condition is clearly documented and clearly connected to your existing service-related diagnosis. That's where we focus.
