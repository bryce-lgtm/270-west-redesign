Services / Appeals

VAC appeals, reconsiderations, and review board support

Help when the answer is "not yet."

If you've received a VAC decision you don't agree with, you have options. We help you understand them — and walk through a strong response, together.

01 — Common situations

When veterans come to us for appeals help

Recognize one of these? We've sat with veterans through each.

Situation 01

"My claim was denied and I don't know why."

We walk through your decision letter line-by-line so you understand exactly what VAC found — and what's missing.

Situation 02

"I got a low rating and I think it should be higher."

Sometimes this calls for a reassessment rather than an appeal. We help you tell the difference.

Situation 03

"I'm out of time for a reconsideration. What now?"

Departmental reviews and VRAB hearings have their own timelines. We help you figure out what's still on the table.
