About 270 West

About 270 West Consulting — VAC claims support for Canadian veterans

True west. For those who served.

270 West Consulting is an independent claims advisory service helping Canadian veterans and their families navigate Veterans Affairs Canada with patience, plain language, and respect.

01 — Why we exist
"In service of your story."

Why 270 West Consulting exists

Too many Canadian veterans give up on the benefits they've earned. Not because they don't qualify — but because the VAC process can feel exhausting, opaque, and easy to mistrust. We've seen veterans walk away after a decade of trying. We've seen families try to navigate it on a partner's behalf and not know where to begin.

We exist to change that. Our role isn't to replace Veterans Affairs Canada — it's to walk beside you and them. To translate paperwork, explain timelines, gather the right documentation, and help you put your strongest possible application in front of VAC.

"Bearing 270°W" is the compass heading for true west — steady, honest, pointed toward what veterans have earned.

02 — A note from our founder
"

I started 270 West because I watched too many veterans give up on benefits they'd earned — not because they didn't qualify, but because no one was sitting beside them to help.

We're not here to fight VAC. We're here to make sure your story shows up clearly when VAC opens your file. To translate the paperwork into plain language. To stand with families, partners, and caregivers when the veteran in their life needs a second set of hands.

Independence matters. Honesty matters. Patience matters most. Privacy. Respect. Support — for as long as you need it. That's the company we set out to build, and we do it all in service of your story.

— Our founder · 270 West Consulting Halifax · Nova Scotia
