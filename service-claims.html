Most VAC denials come down to a missing piece — not a missing eligibility. We help you put together a clear, complete claim from the start.

When veterans come to us for first-time claims

If you're new to the VAC process — or you've started and stalled — you're in the right place.

Situation 01

"I've never applied before and I don't know where to start."

Situation 02
